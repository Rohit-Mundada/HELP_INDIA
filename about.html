About Us

Ever saw a headline and sympathised with the problems of the migrants?

Ever saw a post and wondered what the satisfaction of helping the people reach their home would be like?

Ever wanted to help those stranded workers but couldn’t find and trust a way for it to happen? We bring you an app which connects you to the people in need and lets you help them without anyone’s intervention.

(Tap for more info)

Our App HELP INDIA is a step towards helping those who need it, is a step towards helping India get back on its feet, is a step to serve our nation when it needs it the most. Using our application you will dive into the lives of the stranded migrant workers who are trying their best to reach their home, who don’t have the luxury to complain about the lockdown being ‘boring’.

(Tap anywhere on an empty space to go back)

The HELP INDIA app helps you :

  • Browse through the people in need and select the one according to your capability to help.
  • Connect and help the one in need personally without any outside intervention.
  • Pay 0.0001% back to what the nation has given you.

(Tap for more info)

HELP INDIA is an app for every able Indian to help the people who strive every minute to make it alive through this global pandemic to their home. It is an application for every influencer like Sonu Sood to make a change personally and have something to cherish for their entire lifetime.
