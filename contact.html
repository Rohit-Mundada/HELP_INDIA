Who we are and What we Do:

We are a group of students from BITS pilani, goa campus and NIT surathkal who have had enough of depending upon others to help our nation. We aim to create a transparent NON PROFIT platform to connect the shramiks with helpers, without any outside intervention. We will give you the ability to help them on a personal level without donating us or them any money, you will just make necessary arrangements for them to reach home or any other help they need. WE ARE A GROUP OF STUDENTS WHO HAVE HAD ENOUGH of listening “ yaar these people can only post about stuff in instagram”. We come together to make the change rather than talk about it.
